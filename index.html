Lacrosse

Interesting Facts about Lacrosse

  1. Lacrosse is the fastest sport on two feet

    • This fast paced sport is so quick that fans don't see the ball until it has fallen in the goal
    • Professional athletes can shoot the ball faster than 100 mph

  2. Lacrosse is the fastest growing sport in the nation

    • The number of mens lacrosse players has increased by 110% in the last 12 years
    • The number of womens lacrosse players has increased by 100% in the last 12 years

  3. Lacrosse is the oldest sport in the United States

    • Lacrosse dates back to the year 1100 AD
    • The Native Americans used lacrosse to settle disputes between different tribes
